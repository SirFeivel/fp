### C3: Repoint wallByEdgeKey entries after merge
- **File**: `src/walls.js` (mergeSharedEdgeWalls)
- **Problem**: After absorbing otherWall, only one key deleted from wallByEdgeKey. Stale entries cause surface duplication when later merges find the absorbed wall.
- **Fix**: Replace `wallByEdgeKey.delete(otherKey)` with loop that repoints ALL entries referencing otherWall to the surviving wall.

### C5: Inner corner via line intersection
- **File**: `src/walls.js` (computeFloorWallGeometry, third pass)
- **Problem**: Quad corner fill inner point p3 computed as parallelogram (p1+p2-p4), which is only correct for equal wall thicknesses.
- **Fix**: Added `lineLineIntersect` helper (unbounded, no directional constraint). Added `innerPt` to eps entries. Compute pInner via line-line intersection with parallelogram fallback.
