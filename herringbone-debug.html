What Herringbone Should Look Like:

Classic 90° Herringbone (tiles L×W where L=3, W=1):

The pattern consists of L-shaped pairs arranged diagonally:

      [=H=]         <- Horizontal tile
          |
          |V        <- Vertical tile (connects to H's right end)
          |
      [=H=]|        <- Next H tile (connects to V's bottom)
          |
          |V
          |
      [=H=]|
          |V

Key characteristics:
- Each H tile's RIGHT END meets a V tile's TOP
- Each V tile's BOTTOM meets the next H tile's LEFT END
- This creates a continuous zigzag going down-right
- The pattern appears as interlocking "V" or "L" shapes
